FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What formats do you accept for data? +
We accept CSV, Excel (.xlsx/.xls), Google Sheets (shared link), JSON, and SQL database exports. If you have a different format, just reach out — we likely can work with it.
How does the process work after I choose a plan? +
After you get in touch, we'll have a quick 15-minute call to understand your data and goals. We'll then send a project brief for your approval before starting work. You'll receive updates at key milestones.
Is my data kept confidential? +
Absolutely. Every project is covered by a strict NDA. Your data is never shared, repurposed, or stored beyond the project. We take data privacy very seriously.
Can I request a custom plan not listed here? +
Yes — the packages above are starting points. If your project needs something specific (e.g., ongoing monthly analytics, API integration, or a bespoke dashboard), just contact us and we'll scope a custom plan.
What if I need changes after delivery? +
Each plan includes revision rounds (1–2 depending on tier). Revisions cover scope adjustments within the original brief. Additional revisions or scope changes can be quoted separately.
Do you offer discounts for ongoing work? +
Yes! Clients who commit to monthly or quarterly analytics work receive preferred pricing and priority turnaround. Reach out to discuss a retainer arrangement.
